Protein Synthesis
  • There are 20 amino acids. Like letters in a sentence, it is the order of the amino acids that determine the function of the protein.
  • Transcription: The process of getting the instructions from the DNA

Protein Structure
  • Primary Structure: The order of the amino acids
  • Secondary Structure: The three dimensional shape of secondary parts small sections of a protein
  • Teritary Structure: The shape of a single protein
  • Quaternary Structure: The shape of groups of proteins
